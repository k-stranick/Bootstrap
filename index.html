About the Event



Get ready for an unforgettable night as System of a Down and Korn take the stage for an electrifying performance at MetLife Stadium!

Experience the raw energy of System of a Down, delivering their signature intensity, sharp riffs, and politically charged anthems. Korn brings their unmistakable sound, packed with heavy grooves and haunting melodies that have stood the test of time.

Joining them are Polyphia, blending technical mastery with genre-defying creativity, and Wisp, kicking things off with an explosive opening set. This is more than just a concert—it’s an experience. Don’t miss out!
